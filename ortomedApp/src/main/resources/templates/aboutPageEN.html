Orto-MED

Orto-MED is a team of specialists in the field of orthopedics and traumatology of the musculoskeletal system. Our doctors have the preparation and experience, as well as great contact with the patient. Every day they deal with the prevention, diagnosis and treatment of diseases, inborn injuries and acquired abnormalities of the musculoskeletal system (musculoskeletal system) as well as post-traumatic rehabilitation. We make every effort to ensure that the people under our care are satisfied with the services we provide, they felt comfortable and safe. Our team consists of doctors with extensive experience, working in renowned health care institutions, including in the Department of Orthopedics and Traumatology of the Locomotor System. They constantly improve their qualifications and expand their medical knowledge by participating in courses and training organized in Poland and abroad.
