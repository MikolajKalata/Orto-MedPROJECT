About Orto-MED

Orto-MED is a team of specialists in the field of orthopedics and traumatology of the musculoskeletal system. Our doctors have the preparation and experience, as well as great contact with the patient. Every day we make every effort to ensure that the people under our care are satisfied with the services we provide, feel comfortable and safe.

Experience

Our team consists of doctors with extensive experience, working in renowned health care institutions, including in the Department of Orthopedics and Traumatology of the Locomotor System.
